Tourist Places To Visit In Bangladesh


Sylhet



In northeastern Bengal, on the bank of the River Surma, sits the lovely city of Sylhet in Bangladesh. It serves as the district and divisional headquarters for the districts of Sylhet, Moulvibazar, Habiganj, and Sunamganj.The picture-perfect city has all the elements of a dreamy fairy tale setting, including a picturesque harbour, grand views of the neighbouring mountain range, and vibrant rows of wooden houses. It's not difficult to imagine Sylhet as one of the most gorgeous fairytale settings come to life with its serene, tree-lined parks and cobblestone walkways.

Cox's Bazar


One of Bangladesh’s most famous tourist destinations, Cox’s Bazar, is frequented all year round. For those who want to soak up the sun and sea while vacationing in Cox’s Bazar, the options are almost limitless. Some of the numerous beaches in the area are Kolatoli, Sugandha, Inani, and Laboni Point. Luxurious lodging options abound in this part of the world. Cox’s Bazar is also a shopper’s dream come true. Many of the stores in the area cater to the Burmese market. You may find a wide variety of home décor, apparel, kitchenware, tableware, and cosmetics. Visit one of the many eateries or roadside stands to sample a selection of freshly prepared seafood meals. A wide variety of international cuisines may be found in fine-dining venues. Cox’s Bazar is 397.6 km from Dhaka. However, there are no rail services that go directly from Dhaka to Cox’s Bazar. You may take the railway to Chittagong, after which you will need to hire a vehicle or take the bus to reach your destination. To go to Chittagong, you have more than five different rail options.
